About the Forum

The Accelerate Innovation Forum is a two-day event hosted by Bowie State University (BSU), aimed at bringing together thought leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs. The forum explores cutting-edge advancements in research, technology transfer, and commercialization.

Attend keynotes, workshops, and networking events to learn about the future of technology and build valuable industry connections. For more information, contact the Technology Transfer & Innovation Office at BSUTTIO@bowiestate.edu or ajha@bowiestate.edu.

FAQ

  • Why should leadership prioritize tech transfer and commercialization? Innovation and commercialization drive economic growth and societal impact. Expanding tech transfer efforts maximizes research impact, creates new revenue streams, attracts external funding, and enhances regional and national competitiveness.
  • How does this benefit Bowie State and other USM institutions? This initiative accelerates the translation of research into market-ready technologies, fosters collaboration across USM institutions, enhances visibility of innovation, and strengthens partnerships with industry and government agencies.
  • What is the Regional Tech Transfer Center, and how will it function? The center will serve as a shared resource hub, offering programming, mentorship, and funding opportunities for all USM campuses. It will focus on securing external grants and state funding to support commercialization initiatives.
  • How will this initiative be funded? Funding will be pursued from federal and state sources, including NSF’s GRANTED and TIP funding opportunities, and partnerships with agencies like NIH and industry stakeholders. Regional sponsorships from organizations such as TEDCO and Venturewell will also be sought.
  • How will leadership from other USM institutions be involved? USM leadership will collaborate by sending representatives to participate in discussions, co-hosting future tech transfer events, and contributing to the development of the Regional Tech Transfer Center.
  • What role will sponsors and industry partners play? Sponsors and industry partners will provide resources, mentorship, and funding to support technology transfer goals, fostering stronger university-industry relationships and ensuring innovations reach the marketplace.
